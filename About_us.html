🚀 About Us

     Founded in 1999, Lotus Geo began with photogrammetric compilation services using two AMH analytical plotters and has evolved into a full-spectrum geospatial solutions provider.

Today, we integrate:

➤ Aerial & terrestrial photogrammetry

➤ LiDAR and point cloud processing

➤ Satellite & drone imagery

➤ Advanced GIS workflows


     Our strength lies in combining deep technical expertise, structured processes, and cutting-edge tools to deliver consistent, high-quality results.
