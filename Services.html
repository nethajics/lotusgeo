Orthophoto

     An orthoimage is an aerial or satellite image that has been geometrically corrected (orthorectified) to ensure uniform scale and mapping accuracy. At Lotus Geo Solutions, we offer advanced orthoimage processing services to support precise and reliable geospatial analysis. Our Orthophoto production services include


  • Drone, Satellite & Aerial Orthophotos
  • True Orthophotos
  • 3-Band & 4-Band Outputs
  • Pan-Sharpened Imagery
  • LiDAR-Integrated Orthophotos
