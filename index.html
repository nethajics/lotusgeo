      Lotus Geo is a Geographic Information System (GIS) consulting company that has been pioneering geospatial services in photogrammetry since 1999 and has expanded its services to include LiDAR, GIS, Orthophoto Processing, and Building Information Modelling (BIM). With extensive global experience, we deliver ready-to-use, highly accurate geospatial data supporting diverse industry-focused GIS applications.

We transform complex spatial data into actionable insights—accurately, efficiently, and on time.
